Snowball or avalanche — which clears your debt sooner?

Both roll every freed-up payment onto your next debt. They just differ on the order.

❄️ Snowball

Attack the smallest balance first. You clear whole debts fast, which keeps you motivated — but you may pay a little more interest.

🏔️ Avalanche

Attack the highest APR first. Mathematically cheapest — the least total interest — though the first win can take longer.
